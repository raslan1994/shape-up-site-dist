Welcome to ShapeUp. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.
2. Data Collection and Usage
We respect your privacy and don't collect any of your sensitive or non-sensitive data.Your data stays on your device unless you choose to back it up to cloud services like iCloud Backup, GDrive, or similar. Even then, Shape Up will never have direct access to your data as 3rd party. Shape Up is designed to store all user data locally on the device. We do not collect, store, or share any personal health data on our servers.

We may collect anonymous usage statistics and crash reports to improve app performance and user experience. This data does not include any personally identifiable or health-related information.

Our privacy practices are subject to change. If we decide to modify this policy, we will notify you appropriately before implementing any changes requiring your consent.
